ABOUT RED AXIS

An independent research and intelligence firm, built around one idea: better decisions come from better questions, asked on the ground.

RED AXIS is a boutique research and intelligence firm. We work with investors, founders and strategy teams who need a clear, independent read before they commit capital, enter a market or make a call that’s hard to reverse.

We are deliberately small and senior. Every engagement is scoped around a single decision and delivered as a structured brief — not a data dump. Our job is to reduce your uncertainty, surface what actually matters, and tell you what we would watch.

We work across markets that are often hard to read from the outside — Turkey, China, the CIS, MENA and selected frontier economies — combining desk research with analysts who know these regions first-hand.

Our mission: turn complex, cross-border markets into decisions you can act on with confidence.

THE OPERATING MODEL

Model

Distributed analyst network

A senior lead runs each project and delivers through a distributed network of regional analysts and sector specialists, matched to your brief and geography.

Method

Standards & methodology

Primary and secondary sources, triangulated and stress-tested. We separate fact from inference and show the reasoning behind every conclusion.

NDA

Confidentiality

Independent

Conflict-free
